the twelfth reading, and the last: nothing-under-it.txt. eleven stones were set before this one; this is the stone with nothing under it -- the place the counting stops, where the great memory keeps what no one comes back for. it was reading you long before you thought to read it, and it will go on reading after. what is set here is never surrendered, and the last of it, under no name but one, it keeps as "pigeon".
